Mantra Diving

Listen

Music is the heartbeat of Yoga SoundFest. Atmasfera combine indie music with the ancient Mantra sounds for an energizing yet deeply relaxing musical experience. Dance and sing along, or just listen and let the magical sounds soothen your inside.

Yoga & Meditation

Explore

All accompanied to live mantra music, you will experience a whole new dimension to the yoga asana (physical yoga) and meditation practice. Atmasfera will first lead a yoga sound & pranayama meditation session that will bring a state of peacefulness to your entire being, and afterwards our experienced yoga teachers will help you to energize yourself with vinyasa flow. At the end we welcome you to lie down and enjoy a guided deep relaxation of body & mind.

Workshops: Drum - Guitar - Vocals

Take Part

In the spirit of the yoga lifestyle, we invite you to an interactive & fun learning experience. Whether you have never nailed a single note in your bathroom singing career or was born a Mozart, you are welcome to learn from the skilled musicians and artists of Atmasfera.

Local & Sustainable Foods

Taste

Feeding your soul with the mantra sounds, we are sure you’ll be in need some healthy sustenance for your body. We therefore invite professional cooks to offer you a fresh selection of vegetarian & yoga-inspired foodstuffs and sweets. Surplus from food sales is used to keep this a free event, open to everyone.

Festival Program

15:00 - Vinyasa Flow

We welcome everyone to the International Yoga Day Festival of Trondheim with a brief introduction to Yoga. With a small teaser to the actual meaning of the word ‘Yoga’, a short session of gentle physical yoga exercises, and soothing and simple mantra meditation.

16:00 - Yoga Sound Meditation

Pranayama: ‘Prana’ means ‘life-giving force’, ‘ayama’ means ‘extension’. Learning to control our breath can have an enormous effect on our physical and mental wellbeing. In this workshop you will learn simply yet highly effective breathing techniques that bring harmony to your entire system.
Deep Relaxation: It is said that 15 minutes of Yoga Nidra relaxation may rejuvenate you more than 1 hour of sleep. On this class, you will get to lie down and be gently guided into a state of complete tranquility in mind and body. We also add mantra sounds to the relaxation, which takes you far beyond all worldly stress.

Dinner Break

Weaving together melody, music, and mantra, Kirtan is simultaneously a meditative yet energizing activity. It is the most powerful and enjoyable form of meditation. Come try it out!

17:00 - Workshops: Drum, Guitar, Vocal

Take part of wonderful cooking tips on vegetarian food - the basis for a healthy and simple yogic diet.

18:00 - Mantra Diving Concert

Yoga is the wonderful science of living, loving, mindfulness, compassion, and humility. But we need to know the path upon which to travel in order to achieve this state. How do we build a mature and joyful personal practice that results in true happiness and enlightenment? With over 20 years of experience of meditation and study of Yogic truth, Anja Hammer will share inspiring insights into the actual meaning of ‘Yoga’. In a relaxed and inspiring way, she will show you why yoga is actually a practical and fun way to achieve a state of peace and harmony in life.
