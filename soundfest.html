Mantra Diving

Listen

Music is the heart & soul of Yoga SoundFest - in our main event called 'Mantra Diving', Atmasfera combine an indie music style with ancient Mantras for an energizing yet deeply relaxing music & meditation experience. We invite you to dance and sing along, or otherwise to just listen and embrace the magical mantra sounds in your heart.

Yoga & Meditation

Explore

Experience a whole new dimension to the yoga practice as we combine breath, movement, and live mantra music by Atmasfera. Our experienced yoga teachers will help you to energize and recharge your body with vinyasa flow. With their angel-like voices, Kalindi & Yamuna from Atmasfera will also lead a yoga sound & pranayama meditation session to bring peacefulness to your entire being.

Drum Circle & Guitar/Vocal Workshop

Take Part

In the spirit of the yoga lifestyle, we invite you to an interactive & fun learning experience with the skilled musicians of Atmasfera. With a unique and inspirational teaching style, Drummer Timur will first share a spiritual approach to groove and lead you toward finding your own beat in our Drum Circle - a fun and inclusive workshop for both old and young. We also arrange a guitar/vocal workshop with Dennis and Janardana.

Welcome to Trondheim's most vibrant yoga and music event!
Yoga SoundFest is a place for exploring the depth of the ancient Eastern traditions
- beyond the yoga mat. This mini-festival is an unconventional, joyous, and heart-warming experience
for all kinds of people, in all walks of life. We have especially invited world & yoga music band Atmasfera from Ukraine
to infuse the festival with energy and life. You will have a chance to join;
Mantra Diving, Yoga Flow to live music, Yoga Sound Meditation, Drum Circle, and more.

Let's all come together, celebrate, and lighten up the darkness of autumn
with the ancient yoga sounds - that have the power to enliven our very core.
Our guests from Ukraine, indie yoga band Atmasfera,
will provide you with a magical & creative experience of yoga, music, art, and fun!
