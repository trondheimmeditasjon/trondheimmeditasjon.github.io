Vegetarian Cooking Class

Do you want to learn how to make the most simple and tasty vegetarian food? Or are you already a vegetarian but need some new inspiration?

This evening you will
- Learn how to easily combine a vegetarian meal
- Develop an understanding of how to make healthy food from scratch
- Get a collection of our favorite recipes to use at home

Many people think it's challenging to make a vegetarian meal, but that's certainly not the case! It can actually be very easy and it does not take a lot of time. Our vegetarian chef will provide you with some simple tools and his best advice on how to make tasty dishes. Join a cozy evening with inspiration, good atmosphere, and a tasty dinner at the end
