On the menu, we have Christmas veggie-burgers, nut roast, mashed rutabaga, steamed vegetables,
red cabbage, stewed peas, prunes, pickled red onion, lingonberry jam, and sauce.
For dessert we serve the traditional rice dish "riskrem" with red sauce. After dinner,
there will be live Christmas music. We end the evening with tea/coffee and cake.

All food we serve is plant based, – so no meat, fish, eggs, or dairy.
The event is alcohol-free and you don't have to bring anything; food and beverages are included.
The atmosphere is usually relaxed, so you don't have to put on your galla outfit, but something nice if you want.
This Christmas Party is suitable for families :)
