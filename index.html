YOGA SOUNDFEST

29. SEPT - 7. OKT 2018
Yoga SoundFest is a place for exploring the depth of the ancient Eastern traditions - beyond the yoga mat. This mini-festival is a beautiful, heart-warming experience for all kinds of people, in all walks of life. We welcome bankers, nurses, students, teens, seniors; We welcome you. Our guests from Ukraine, indie yoga band Atmasfera,
will provide you with a magical & creative experience of yoga, music, art, and fun.
