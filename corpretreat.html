INNER PEACE RETREAT

Storbuan Økosamfunn

Relax, take a deep breath of fresh mountain air, and savour the tranquility. On our wellness retreats you can experience a deep rejuvenation as you release layers and layers of tension. The retreat is held in a beautiful location in the rolling hills of the south Trøndelag countryside, 1.5h from Trondheim; Storbuan Økosamfunn - a peaceful oasis from the outside world.

During the retreat you will learn & develop many skills that can be easily implemented in your daily life to help to recreate the sublime peace you will be experiencing throughout your stay. We will be learning calming yoga breathing, a variety of meditation practices, and enjoying simple yoga classes perfect for all levels. All these practices take you into an energized yet relaxed mood in your body and mind, and effectively removes any stress or anxiety.

You will also get the chance to deeply relax & recharge in our Yoga Sound Therapy sessions. This is a group meditation where live music is combined with ancient yoga sounds. We end each day with a soothing lying-down deep relaxation. The delicious vegetarian meals are always a positive surprise for our guests, especially those who are not used to a meatless meal. 

Don’t be surprised if you come home with a lightness of spirit and a smile on your face, nourishing this wonderful experience in your heart.
