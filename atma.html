ATMASFERA IN TRONDHEIM

World Indie Concert

SATURDAY 29 SEPTEMBER 2018

Atmasfera combine indie music with ethno-motifs from the entire world. They have a unique intensity of sound in their dynamic melodies. In Atmasfera's music, you find an interesting fusion of modern western tendencies and ancient eastern culture, with a touch of Ukrainian folklore.

Yoga SoundFest

SUNDAY 30 SEPTEMBER 2018

Let's all come together, celebrate, and lighten up the darkness of autumn
with the ancient yoga sounds - that have the power to enliven our very being! Our special guests from Ukraine, indie yoga band Atmasfera, make this a completely magical experience of yoga, music, art, and fun.
